NHL Bracket Challenge 2025 - Rules & Instructions

Welcome to the NHL Bracket Challenge! Test your hockey knowledge by predicting the outcome of the 2025 Stanley Cup Playoffs.

Below are the rules and instructions for participating in our bracket challenge.

  • Select your dream lineup with a budget of $1,000,000

  • Points are awarded based on your players' playoff performance:
  • Skaters: 1 point per actual playoff point (goals + assists)
  • Goalies: 1 point per win, plus bonus points for shutouts

üîÆ Predictions Challenge Rules

  • Make predictions across 8 different categories:
  • Conn Smythe winner (playoff MVP)
  • Top goal scorer

  • Top defenseman scorer
  • Top U23 scorer
  • Top Finnish player
  • Goalie with best GAA
  • Player with most penalty minutes
  • 10 points awarded for each correct prediction

How to Play

1. Create Your Bracket

Visit the "Bracket" page to make your playoff predictions:

  • Select winners for each first-round matchup
  • Continue selecting winners through each round
  • Choose the number of games each series will last (4-7)
  • Don't forget to pick your Stanley Cup Champion!
  • Click "Save Picks" when you're done

2. Build Your Lineup

Visit the "Lineup" page to build your dream team:

  • Select players to fill each position (L, C, R, LD, RD, G)
  • Stay within the $1,000,000 salary cap
  • Higher-rated players cost more but may score more points
  • Click "Save Lineup" when you're done

3. Make Predictions

Visit the "Predictions" page to make your category predictions:

  • Select players for each award/statistical category
  • You can add multiple players to each category (ranked by confidence)
  • Click "Save Predictions" when you're done

4. Track Your Progress

Once the playoffs begin, you can:

  • Check the leaderboard to see your ranking
  • Visit your dashboard for detailed scoring breakdown
  • View other participants' brackets, lineups, and predictions

Prizes

The top finishers will receive the following prizes:

  • 1st Place: NHL Jersey of choice + Championship Trophy
  • 2nd Place: $50 NHL Shop Gift Card
  • 3rd Place: Official NHL Puck signed by Finnish NHL players

Tips & Strategies

  • Research team matchups and recent performance
  • Consider injuries to key players
  • Look at regular season head-to-head records
  • Don't ignore special teams performance (power play & penalty kill)
  • Goaltending can make or break a playoff run
  • Home ice advantage matters in Game 7s
